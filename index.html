About me

My journey in tech began after obtaining a Masters degree in Math and Computer Science. However, in 2018, driven by my desire for new opportunities and personal growth, I made the life-changing decision to relocate to the vibrant city of New York.

Motivated by desire to explore culture, I took on a part-time gig at a local restaurant while freelancing. This unique experience not only sharpened my soft skills and improved my interpersonal communication but also gave me valuable insights into client-oriented businesses. After the pandemic subsided, I was excited to pursue my dream of becoming a Software Engineer!

Throughout my journey, I've discovered that I'm an enthusiastic and quick learner with a diligent and persistent nature. My passion for technology drives me. If you're looking for a dedicated software engineer, I'd be delighted to connect and discuss how I can contribute to your project. Let's collaborate to create something amazing together!
