About me

Having successfully earned my Masters degree in Math and Computer Science, I worked as a teacher of computer science for two years. In 2018, fueled by the desire for new opportunities and personal growth, I made a life-changing decision to relocate to the vibrant city of New York.

Motivated by a strong desire to explore culture and language, I fully immersed myself in the dynamic hospitality industry. This experience allowed me to significantly enhance my soft skills and interpersonal communication. After the pandemic subsided, I was tremendously excited to pursue my dream of becoming a Software Engineer!

Let's collaborate to create something amazing together!
