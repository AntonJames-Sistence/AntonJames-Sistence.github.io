About me

My journey in tech began with the attainment of a Master's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science. Afterward, I worked as a computer science teacher for two years. In 2018, driven by my desire for new opportunities and personal growth, I made the decision to relocate to New York.

Motivated by a desire to explore culture, I took on a part-time gig at a local restaurant while freelancing. This unique experience not only sharpened my soft skills and improved my interpersonal communication but also provided me with valuable insights into client-oriented businesses. After the pandemic subsided, I was excited to further enhance my skills at App Academy, one of the most renowned software engineering bootcamps in the country.

Throughout my journey, I've discovered that I'm an enthusiastic and quick learner with a diligent and persistent nature. My passion for delivering meaningful impact drives me. If you're in search of a dedicated Front-end software engineer, I'd be delighted to connect and discuss how I can contribute to your project. Let's collaborate to create something amazing together!
