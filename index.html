About me

I have a Masters in math and computer science which has given me a solid background in these subjects, and has laid a solid foundation for my journey in tech. After teaching computer science in Kiev, Ukraine for two years, I made life-changing decision to move to New York in 2018.

After the pandemic ended, I was very excited to go ahead and pursue my dream of becoming a software engineer! I strongly believe in peer-learning, and the rigorous in-person classroom experience of App Academy held a lot of appeal for me. I was eager to embrace the opportunity to undergo intensive training and education, honing my coding skills and expanding my knowledge in new technologies.

Throughout my academic career, I have realized that I am an ardent and quick learner, hardworking and persistent and my love for technology makes me look at the ever evolving field of software technology with a lot of excitement. If you're looking for a software engineer with a strong background, I'd love to connect and discuss how I can contribute to your projects. Feel free to reach out and let's create something amazing together!
